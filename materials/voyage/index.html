Three ways to see each mission

Every spacecraft has the same three lenses. Switch modes to change what you're looking at — the object itself, the journey it took, or the view from on board.

Model

Rotate the actual NASA 3D model. Drag, zoom, see the hardware up close.

Path

See the stylized trajectory between planets — Voyager's Grand Tour, Cassini's gravity-assist loop.

Ride

An on-rails ride alongside the spacecraft, slowing near each planetary flyby.

Space history you can hold in your hands

Most space resources are flat: a photo here, a Wikipedia entry there. Voyage puts the actual NASA 3D models in front of you, in chronological order, so the leap from Friendship 7 to JWST becomes something you can feel.

  • Self-hosted models — no signup, no slow CDN
  • A trivia hook for every mission — great for class discussion
